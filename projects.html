School Projects

Medinar

Medinar is a powerful scheduling app developed with Firebase at its core. Nurses create and change schedules for elderly patients who view their schedule for the day

Medinar was developed with user experience in mind. From day one, Medinar was designed to be easy to use for elderly patients

Studr

Studr is an app that a makes it easy to find and join study groups in your area, or around your university. It also makes it easy to create your own groups and invite your friends

I used swift to design a iOS application for front end, and Parse to design the backend. I also used some cocoa-pod frameworks

Studr is a large progect, and we plan on continuing working on it in the future. We plan on implementing QR code mechanics, a completely functional social system, and Google map support. We also intend on using the Uber api to give users the ability to find quick and reliable transportation

NewSnap

NewSnap is designed to revolutionize the way you recieve news

It will remove the clutter within news articles and current events. The news recieved is user-based, and will be moderated by users. Users will also be able to judge credibility, and the credibility of a post will be ranked. Users who accurately rate posts will be rewarded with an increased weight in voting

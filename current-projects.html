Newsnap

NewSnap is designed to revolutionize the way you recieve news

It will remove the clutter within news articles and current events

The news recieved is user-based, and will be moderated by users

Users will also be able to judge credibility, and the credibility of a post will be ranked

Users who accurately rate posts will be rewarded with an increased weight in voting
